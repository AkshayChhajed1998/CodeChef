Rules

Round 3

This round will be Buzzer Round with questions flashing, which will be a mixture of the previous round and Negative marking is applicable.

  1. Round will consist of 1 hour 10 minutes.
  2. In first 10 minutes, we will display the questions and the team which will press the buzzer first will be alloted that question.
  3. Next question will be popped once a question is alloted to a team.
  4. Each team can take maximum 5 questions, there is no minimum limit for questions.
  5. Hint for a question may be hidden in the problem statement itself.
  6. Each team has to code all the questions which they have selected.
  7. If team is unable to solve a question or solved question(s) is/are wrong then they will be negatively marked with -50 points.
  8. If the team codes the problem correctly then they will be given 100 points.
  9. Teams will have 1 hour to code.
